What Is This?

Find the Koroks hidden all over campus! Each Korok has a QR code to scan, which will increment your score.
Compete against your fellow students to find the most Koroks!

All Koroks will be found outside, and within the bounds shown above. Don't go searching anywhere you shouldn't be; Koroks probably aren't hiding there.

The top three players by the end of ??? will win ???!

For announcements, conversation, and/or questions about the event, we have a discord server!





What's New?

This is the second Korok Hunt! As such, there are a few small changes from last year:
- The bounds have been made smaller to no longer include Freshman Hill.
- The Koroks will be released in waves of around 30 Koroks every 2 or so weeks
- Each batch of released Koroks will be in a particular area of campus, so that you don't have to re-search anywhere after new waves





Cheating Notice

Don't ruin the fun for everyone else.
Removing or moving Koroks from their original location, as well as scanning Koroks that have been removed will result in disqualification.
